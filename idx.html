Cyber Security Threats Awareness Portal

Project Overview & Research Objectives

This comprehensive digital platform serves as the official, heavily researched output created explicitly for academic purposes by the ICT 12 ST. VERONICA students from Lian Institute Inc. The overarching objective of this extensive website is to systematically educate end-users, students, and professionals alike on the rapidly expanding landscape of modern digital threats. By synthesizing vast amounts of related literature and authoritative cybersecurity frameworks, we aim to provide actionable, fact-based strategies that empower individuals to proactively defend their data.

What Does This Website Contain?

Within this portal, you will explore highly detailed sections covering Malware, Network Interceptions, and Social Engineering. Furthermore, we have integrated an interactive AI chatbot named CyVoid, meticulously programmed with our gathered research data to answer your specific queries in real-time. This project represents our commitment to digital safety and technical excellence in the field of Information and Communications Technology.
